Apps-ka Forex Trading-ka ee ugu Fiican

1. MetaTrader 5 (MT5)

Platform-ka ugu caansan ee falanqaynta suuqyada iyo ganacsiyada (trades). Waa mid deggan oo u ogolaanaya noocyo kala duwan oo suuq ah.

Aamin & Caalami Download

2. TradingView

App-ka ugu fiican ee lagu sameeyo Charts-ka, isticmaalida indicators, iyo la wadaagista falanqaynta bulshada weyn ee traders-ka.

Falanqayn Farsamo Download

3. Investing.com

Wuxuu ku siinayaa wararka suuqa Forex (Economic Calendar), qiimaha waqtiga dhabta ah, iyo qalabka aasaasiga ah.

Wararka Suuqa Download

4. FXStreet

App gaar u ah wararka degdegga ah iyo falanqaynta suuqyada ee saamayn ku leh Forex, si aad ula socoto dhaqdhaqaaqa suuqa.

Digniino Degdeg ah Download

5. Myfxbook

Wuxuu kaa caawinayaa la socoshada akoonnadaada ganacsi, falanqaynta waxqabadkaaga iyo helista tirokoobyada muhiimka ah.

La Socoshada Akoonka Download

6. Finviz

App loogu talagalay in lagu shaandheeyo (screener) suuqyada si loo helo fursadaha ganacsi ee ugu muhiimsan, iyadoo la adeegsanayo filters kala duwan.

Shaandhaynta Suuqa Download

7. Yahoo Finance

Xog suuq, qiimaha saamiyada iyo lacagaha, wararka maaliyadeed ee guud, oo loogu talagalay in lala socdo xaaladda suuqa.

Xogta Suuqa Download

8. Economic Calendar

Wuxuu si gaar ah diiradda u saarayaa dhacdooyinka dhaqaalaha adduunka (news events) ee saamayn ku leh lamaanayaasha lacagaha (currency pairs).

Jadwalka Dhaqaalaha Download

9. Binance App

Waxaa loo isticmaali karaa in lagu kala iibsado cryptocurrencies, iyo sidoo kale isdhexgalka suuqyada qaar ee Forex iyo derivatives.

Crypto & Derivatives Download

10. DailyForex

Wuxuu bixiyaa falanqayn, dib u eegis ku saabsan brokers, iyo macluumaad waxbarasho oo ku saabsan Forex trading-ka maalinlaha ah.

Waxbarasho & Falanqayn Download

11. MetaTrader 4 (MT4)

Waa platform-kii caadiga ahaa ee hore. Waxay ku fiican tahay ka ganacsiga Forex oo leh awood falanqayn oo xooggan.

Aasaasiga Ganacsiga Forex Download

12. eToro

Platform caan ku ah trading copy, kaas oo kuu ogolaanaya inaad la socoto traders kale oo aad ka baratid xeeladahooda.

Copy Trading Download
